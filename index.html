interesting Facts

From pharaohs to coral reefs, Egypt packs a punch. Pyramids pierce the desert sky, the Nile nourishes life, and vibrant culture thrums through bustling cities. Dive deep into history, savor delicious cuisine, and get lost in timeless allure – Egypt awaits.

Rich Legacy

Home to hieroglyphics, the world's oldest written language, and impressive achievements in astronomy, mathematics, and architecture.

Diverse Population

Over 99% of Egyptians are of Arabic descent, with various minorities and religious groups like Coptic Christians.

Nile River Lifeline

Stretching over 1,000 km, the Nile River nourishes the fertile Nile Valley, sustaining most of the population and agriculture.

First recorded peace treaty

Between Ramses II of Egypt and Hattusili III of the Hittite Empire in 1284 B.C.
